## Meeting Cadence & Reporting Schedule

### Daily

| Meeting | Participants | Purpose | Duration |
|---------|--------------|---------|----------|
| Engineering Standup | Engineering Team | Progress, blockers | 15 min |
| Sales Huddle | Sales Team | Pipeline review | 15 min |

### Weekly

| Meeting | Participants | Purpose | Duration |
|---------|--------------|---------|----------|
| Executive Sync | All C-Suite + Huzaifa | Company updates, decisions | 60 min |

### Decision Matrix (RAPID)

## Escalation Path

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                              ESCALATION HIERARCHY                                    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Level 1: Team Lead
    │
    │  If unresolved within 24 hours
    ▼
Level 2: Department Head (CTO/COO/CFO/CMO/Academy Lead)
    │
    │  If unresolved within 48 hours or cross-functional
    ▼
Level 3: CEO
    │
    │  If strategic/legal/financial risk
    ▼
Level 4: Board of Directors
```

### Escalation Triggers

| Trigger | Escalate To | Timeline |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Customer complaint (critical) | COO | Immediate |
| Security incident | CTO + CEO | Immediate |
| Legal/compliance issue | CFO + CEO | Immediate |
| Revenue at risk (over €10K) | CEO | 24 hours |
| Team conflict | Department Head | 48 hours |
| Project delay (over 1 week) | Department Head | 48 hours |

## Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Response Time |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| **Slack** | Day-to-day communication | 4 hours |
| **Email** | Formal communication, external | 24 hours |
| **Video Call** | Meetings, discussions | Scheduled |
| **Phone** | Urgent matters | Immediate |
| **Notion/Docs** | Documentation, async collaboration | 48 hours |

### Channel Guidelines

- **#general** - Company-wide announcements
- **#engineering** - Technical discussions
- **#sales** - Pipeline and deal updates
- **#support** - Customer issues
- **#random** - Social, non-work chat
- **DMs** - Private, sensitive matters
