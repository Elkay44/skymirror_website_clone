| Jerone Lebadie (CMO) | Common B | 1 vote/share |
| Employee Option Pool | Options | N/A |
| Advisor Pool | Options | N/A |

**Note:** Specific share counts and equity percentages are confidential and shared with investors under NDA only.

**Rationale for Founder-Led Structure:**
- Lukman Ibrahim is the sole funder of all company operations
- Originated and developed all core business ideas (CheckMet, Traquiva, Academy)
- Bears full financial risk
- Provides strategic direction across all business units
- Maintains control for decisive leadership

**2. Share Classes**

| Class | Description | Rights |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Common A | Founder shares (CEO) | Full voting, board seat, anti-dilution protection |
| Common B | Co-founder shares | Full voting, proportional representation |
| Options | Employee/Advisor pool | Vest over 4 years, 1-year cliff |

**3. Transfer Restrictions**

- **Right of First Refusal (ROFR):** Company and then other shareholders have right to purchase shares before any external sale
- **Lock-up Period:** 2 years from grant date for all founders
- **Drag-Along Rights:** Majority shareholder (CEO) can require all shareholders to participate in approved sale
- **Tag-Along Rights:** Minority shareholders can participate in any sale by majority shareholder

**4. Decision-Making**

| Decision Type | Approval Required |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Ordinary business | CEO approval |
| Annual budget | CEO + CFO |
| Equity grants | CEO + Board |
| Major contracts (over €100K) | CEO |
| Fundraising | CEO + Board |
| Sale of company | 75% shareholder approval |
| Amendment to shareholder agreement | 75% shareholder approval |
