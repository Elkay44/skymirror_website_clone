## Risk Mitigation

### Launch Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|------------|-------|
| Technical issues at launch | Extensive QA, staged rollout | CTO |
| Low initial adoption | Pre-launch waitlist, launch incentives | CMO |
| Customer support overload | Scaled support team, self-service docs | COO |
| Cash flow constraints | Conservative spending, milestone-based hiring | CFO |

### Growth Risks

| Risk | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|------------|-------|
| Competition | Continuous innovation, customer lock-in | CEO |
| Churn | Proactive success management, feature development | COO |
| Hiring challenges | Employer branding, competitive packages | CEO |
| Market changes | Diversified product portfolio, agile development | CTO |
