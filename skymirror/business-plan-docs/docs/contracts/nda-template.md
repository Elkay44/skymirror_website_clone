### RECITALS

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship concerning [DESCRIBE PURPOSE] (the "Purpose"). In connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose Confidential Information to the other Party.

---

### 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**1.1** "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"), whether orally, in writing, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

- (a) Business plans, strategies, and forecasts
- (b) Financial information and projections
- (c) Customer and supplier information
- (d) Technical data, designs, and specifications
- (e) Software, source code, and algorithms
- (f) Trade secrets and know-how
- (g) Marketing plans and pricing information
- (h) Employee and personnel information
- (i) Any information marked "Confidential" or similar designation

**1.2** Confidential Information does not include information that:

- (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party
- (b) Was rightfully known to the Receiving Party before disclosure
- (c) Is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction
- (d) Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- (e) Is required to be disclosed by law, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt notice

---

### 2. OBLIGATIONS

**2.1 Protection.** The Receiving Party shall:

- (a) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care
- (b) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- (c) Limit disclosure to employees and advisors with a need to know who are bound by confidentiality obligations
- (d) Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent

**2.2 No License.** Nothing in this Agreement grants any license or rights to Confidential Information except as expressly stated.

**2.3 No Obligation.** This Agreement does not obligate either Party to:
- (a) Disclose any particular information
- (b) Enter into any further agreement
- (c) Proceed with any transaction

---

### 3. TERM

**3.1** This Agreement shall remain in effect for [2] years from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by either Party with [30] days' written notice.

**3.2** Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for [3] years, or indefinitely for trade secrets.

---

### 4. RETURN OF INFORMATION

Upon termination or request, the Receiving Party shall:
- (a) Return or destroy all Confidential Information
- (b) Certify destruction in writing if requested
- (c) Retain only copies required by law or internal compliance

---

### 5. REMEDIES

**5.1** The Parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages are inadequate.

**5.2** The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief without proving actual damages or posting bond.

**5.3** Remedies are cumulative and not exclusive.

---

### 6. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**6.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by Hungarian law.

**6.2 Dispute Resolution.** Disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in Budapest, Hungary.

**6.3 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality.

**6.4 Amendments.** Amendments must be in writing signed by both Parties.

**6.5 Assignment.** Neither Party may assign without consent.

**6.6 Severability.** Invalid provisions shall be modified to be enforceable.

**6.7 Counterparts.** This Agreement may be signed in counterparts.

## ONE-WAY NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**(Skymirror as Disclosing Party)**

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE]

**BETWEEN:**

**Skymirror Kft.** ("Disclosing Party")

**AND:**

**[RECIPIENT NAME]** ("Receiving Party")

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Disclosing Party wishes to disclose certain Confidential Information to the Receiving Party for the purpose of [DESCRIBE PURPOSE] (the "Purpose").

---

### 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by the Disclosing Party, including:
- Business plans and strategies
- Technical information and source code
- Customer and financial information
- Any information marked confidential

---

### 3. OBLIGATIONS

The Receiving Party shall:
- (a) Keep Confidential Information strictly confidential
- (b) Use only for the Purpose
- (c) Not disclose to third parties without written consent
- (d) Limit internal disclosure to those with need to know
- (e) Protect with reasonable security measures
- (f) Return or destroy upon request

---

### 4. EXCLUSIONS

Obligations do not apply to information that:
- (a) Is publicly available
- (b) Was known before disclosure
- (c) Is independently developed
- (d) Is received from a third party without restriction
- (e) Is required by law to be disclosed

---

### 5. TERM

This Agreement remains in effect for [2] years. Confidentiality obligations survive for [3] years after termination.

---

### 6. NO LICENSE

No license or rights are granted except as expressly stated.

---

### 7. REMEDIES

The Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief for breach.

---

### 8. GENERAL

- Governed by Hungarian law
- Disputes resolved by arbitration in Budapest
- Amendments require written agreement

## NDA QUICK REFERENCE

### When to Use Each Template

| Situation | Template |
|-----------|----------|
| Partnership discussions | Mutual NDA |
| Investor meetings | Mutual NDA |
| Vendor evaluations | One-Way (Skymirror disclosing) |
| Customer demos | One-Way (Skymirror disclosing) |
| New employees | Employee Confidentiality |
| Contractors | Contractor Agreement (includes NDA) |

### Key Terms to Customize

| Term | Standard | Negotiable Range |
|------|----------|------------------|
| Duration | 2 years | 1-5 years |
| Survival | 3 years | 2-5 years |
| Notice period | 30 days | 14-60 days |
| Jurisdiction | Hungary | Negotiable |

### Red Flags to Watch For

- ❌ Unlimited liability
- ❌ No carve-outs for public information
- ❌ Perpetual obligations (except trade secrets)
- ❌ Overly broad definition of confidential
- ❌ No return/destruction provisions
