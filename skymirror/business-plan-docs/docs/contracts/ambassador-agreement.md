# Skymirror Academy Ambassador Agreement

This Ambassador Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Skymirror Kft.** ("Company") and the undersigned individual ("Ambassador").

## 1. Scope of Role
The Ambassador agrees to represent Skymirror Academy at their designated university or community. This includes:
- Promoting Skymirror Academy courses and bootcamps.
- Organizing or facilitating on-campus workshops or info sessions.
- Sharing marketing materials through approved digital and physical channels.

## 3. Brand & Conduct
- **Representation:** The Ambassador must represent Skymirror professionally and accurately.
- **No Guarantees:** The Ambassador shall not guarantee employment or specific salaries to prospective students.
- **Compliance:** The Ambassador must follow all university policies regarding promotion and events.

## 4. Confidentiality
The Ambassador may have access to non-public information about Skymirror's roadmap or pricing. This information must be kept confidential and not shared with third parties without written consent.

## 5. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with 7 days' written notice. Upon termination, the Ambassador must cease all representation of Skymirror and return any Company property.

## 6. Intellectual Property
All marketing materials, decks, and branding provided by Skymirror remain the property of Skymirror Kft.
