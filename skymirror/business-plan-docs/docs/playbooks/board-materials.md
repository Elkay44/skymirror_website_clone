# Board & Investor Materials

## Overview

This playbook provides templates and guidelines for board meetings, investor updates, and stakeholder communications. These materials follow industry best practices for corporate governance and investor relations.

## Board Meeting Framework

### Meeting Cadence

| Meeting Type | Frequency | Duration | Attendees |
|--------------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Board Meeting | Quarterly | 2-3 hours | Board + C-suite |
| Board Call | Monthly | 30-60 min | Board + CEO |
| Committee Meetings | As needed | 1 hour | Committee members |
| Annual Meeting | Annually | Half day | All stakeholders |

### Standard Agenda (Quarterly Board Meeting)

| Time | Topic | Presenter | Duration |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| 0:00 | Call to Order & Approval of Minutes | Chair | 5 min |
| 0:05 | CEO Update | Lukman Ibrahim | 20 min |
| 0:25 | Financial Review | Abdulwahab A (CFO) | 20 min |
| 0:45 | Product & Technology | Eric (CTO) | 15 min |
| 1:00 | Sales & Marketing | Jerone Lebadie (CMO) | 15 min |
| 1:15 | Operations | Dr. Loveday (COO) | 10 min |
| 1:25 | Break | - | 10 min |
| 1:35 | Strategic Discussion | All | 30 min |
| 2:05 | Committee Reports | Committee Chairs | 15 min |
| 2:20 | Executive Session | Board only | 20 min |
| 2:40 | Action Items & Close | Chair | 10 min |

| Seat | Type | Status | Notes |

| 2 | Founder | Filled | [Co-founder] |
| 3 | Investor | Post-Seed | Lead investor |
| 4 | Independent | Year 2 | Industry expert |
| 5 | Independent | Year 3 | Finance/governance |

### Board Committees (Post-Series A)

| Committee | Purpose | Members |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Audit | Financial oversight | 2 Independent + CFO |
| Compensation | Executive pay, equity | 2 Independent + CEO |
| Nominating | Board composition | 2 Independent + 1 Founder |

### Board Observer Rights

Investors with observer rights may attend board meetings but cannot vote.

## Meeting Preparation Checklist

### 2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Confirm meeting date/time with all attendees
- [ ] Book meeting room or video conference
- [ ] Begin drafting board deck

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Complete board deck draft
- [ ] CEO review and approval
- [ ] Distribute board package to directors
- [ ] Confirm attendance

### 2 Days Before
- [ ] Final deck updates
- [ ] Prepare any supplementary materials
- [ ] Test video/audio equipment
- [ ] Brief presenters

### Day Of
- [ ] Arrive early / test technology
- [ ] Have backup copies of materials
- [ ] Designate note-taker
- [ ] Record action items in real-time

### Within 1 Week After
- [ ] Distribute meeting minutes
- [ ] Send action item tracker
- [ ] Follow up on decisions needed
- [ ] Schedule next meeting
