# Academy Instructor Compensation Framework

## Overview

This framework defines the compensation structure, requirements, and career progression for Skymirror Academy instructors, with special focus on the intern-to-instructor pipeline.

**Target Audience:** Former interns, external instructors, curriculum developers  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Instructor Categories

### 1. Intern Instructors (Months 5-6 of Internship)

**Eligibility:**
- Minimum 4/5 performance rating in Month 4 evaluation
- Completed "Teaching Fundamentals" training
- Strong technical skills in teaching area
- Approval from Program Lead and Academy Lead

**Time Allocation:**
- Maximum 20% of work time (8 hours/week)
- Balanced with product development work

**Compensation:**
- **Per Session:** Competitive rate for 2-4 hour sessions (details in offer letter)
- **Curriculum Development:** Hourly rate for content creation
- **Session Preparation:** Included in session rate
- **Payment:** Within 15 days of session delivery

**Responsibilities:**
- Deliver 5-10 Academy sessions during Months 5-6
- Prepare teaching materials and exercises
- Grade assignments and provide feedback
- Attend instructor training sessions
- Collaborate with Academy team

---

### 2. Extended Intern Instructors (Months 7-12)

**Eligibility:**
- Successfully completed 6-month internship
- Accepted extension offer
- Demonstrated teaching ability in Months 5-6
- Minimum 4/5 overall performance rating

**Time Allocation:**
- 30% of work time (12 hours/week)
- 70% product development work

**Compensation:**
- **Base Stipend:** Enhanced monthly rate (includes teaching time)
- **Teaching Bonus:** Additional per-session compensation (beyond base allocation)
- **Curriculum Development:** Hourly rate for content development
- **Quarterly Performance Bonus:** Performance-based quarterly incentive

**Responsibilities:**
- Deliver 10-20 Academy sessions per month
- Develop new curriculum content
- Mentor current interns
- Assist with bootcamp programs
- Contribute to Academy strategy

---

### 3. Part-Time Academy Instructors (Post-Internship)

**Eligibility:**
- Completed Skymirror internship (6+ months)
- Strong teaching evaluations
- Subject matter expertise
- Available for regular schedule

**Engagement Types:**

#### A. Regular Part-Time (10-20 hours/week)

**Compensation:**
- **Hourly Rate:** Competitive market rate
- **Session Rate:** Per-session compensation based on complexity
- **Monthly Retainer:** Optional retainer for guaranteed availability
- **Curriculum Development:** Hourly rate for content creation

**Benefits:**
- Flexible schedule
- Remote work options
- Professional development budget (â‚¬50/month)
- Access to Company resources

#### B. Freelance/Per-Session

**Compensation:**
- **Session Rate:** Premium per-session rate
- **Preparation:** Hourly rate for preparation time (capped per session)
- **Curriculum Development:** Competitive hourly rate
- **Workshop/Bootcamp:** Daily rate for intensive programs

**Benefits:**
- Complete flexibility
- Project-based engagement
- No minimum commitment

---

### 4. Full-Time Academy Instructors

**Eligibility:**
- Proven teaching track record (50+ sessions)
- Strong technical expertise
- Curriculum development experience
- Leadership potential

**Compensation Package:**

**Base Salary:**
- Junior Instructor: Competitive entry-level salary
- Instructor: Mid-level market rate
- Senior Instructor: Senior-level compensation
- Lead Instructor: Leadership-level compensation

**Additional Compensation:**
- **Performance Bonus:** Annual performance-based bonus (10-20% of base)
- **Session Bonus:** Additional per-session compensation (beyond base load)
- **Curriculum Development:** Included in base or hourly rate
- **Private Workshops:** Daily workshop rate
- **Corporate Training:** Premium daily rate for corporate programs

**Benefits:**
- Health insurance (company-covered)
- Professional development budget
- Conference attendance budget
- Equipment: Laptop, monitor, accessories
- Flexible work schedule
- Remote work options
- Equity options (after 1 year)
- Paid vacation: 25 days/year
- Sick leave: 15 days/year

**Responsibilities:**
- Deliver 15-25 sessions per month
- Develop and update curriculum
- Mentor instructors and interns
- Conduct student evaluations
- Contribute to Academy strategy
- Represent Academy at events

## Compensation Structure Details

### Session-Based Compensation

**Session Types & Rates:**

| Session Type | Duration | Intern Rate | Part-Time Rate | Full-Time Bonus |
|--------------|----------|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Lecture** | 2 hours | Base rate | Standard rate | Bonus rate |
| **Workshop** | 4 hours | Enhanced rate | Premium rate | Bonus rate |
| **Bootcamp Day** | 6-8 hours | Daily rate | Premium daily | Bonus rate |
| **1:1 Mentoring** | 1 hour | Hourly rate | Standard hourly | Bonus rate |
| **Code Review** | 1 hour | Hourly rate | Standard hourly | Bonus rate |
| **Guest Lecture** | 2 hours | Enhanced rate | Premium rate | N/A |

**Preparation Time:**
- First-time session: Up to 4 hours paid
- Repeat session: Up to 1 hour paid
- Major curriculum update: Up to 3 hours paid

**Grading & Feedback:**
- Included in session rate for fewer than 20 students
- Additional compensation per student for more than 20 students

---

### Curriculum Development Compensation

**Development Rates:**

| Activity | Intern Rate | Part-Time Rate | Full-Time |
|----------|-------------|----------------|-----------|
| **New Course Creation** | Hourly rate | Premium hourly | Included in base |
| **Course Update** | Hourly rate | Standard hourly | Included in base |
| **Exercise Creation** | Hourly rate | Standard hourly | Included in base |
| **Video Production** | Enhanced hourly | Premium hourly | Included + bonus |
| **Assessment Design** | Hourly rate | Standard hourly | Included in base |

**Deliverables Required:**
- Detailed course outline
- Lecture slides/materials
- Hands-on exercises
- Assessment questions
- Student resources
- Instructor guide

**Quality Standards:**
- Peer review required
- Academy Lead approval
- Student feedback Greater than 4/5
- Regular updates (quarterly)

---

### Performance Bonuses

**Quarterly Bonuses (All Instructor Types):**

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Student satisfaction: 40%
- Teaching effectiveness: 30%
- Curriculum contribution: 20%
- Professionalism: 10%

**Bonus Structure:**
- Intern Instructors: Performance-based quarterly bonus
- Extended Interns: Enhanced quarterly bonus
- Part-Time: Quarterly performance incentive
- Full-Time: 10-20% of quarterly base salary

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 4/5 student rating
- Delivered all scheduled sessions
- Met curriculum deadlines
- No professional conduct issues

---

### Special Compensation Opportunities

**1. Corporate Training**

**Rates:**
- Half-day (4 hours): Competitive half-day rate
- Full-day (8 hours): Premium full-day rate
- Multi-day programs: Enhanced daily rate for extended programs

**Requirements:**
- Customized curriculum
- Professional presentation
- Follow-up materials
- Client satisfaction Greater than 4.5/5

**2. Conference Speaking**

**Compensation:**
- Conference fee covered
- Travel & accommodation
- Speaking fee (based on event)
- Preparation time: Hourly rate (capped)

**3. Content Creation**

**Blog Posts:**
- Technical tutorial: Per-article rate
- Case study: Enhanced article rate
- Video tutorial: Premium content rate

**Video Courses:**
- Per hour of content: Premium hourly rate
- Editing included
- Royalties: Percentage of sales (10-20%)

**4. Mentorship Programs**

**1:1 Mentoring:**
- Competitive hourly rate
- Package options available
- Career coaching focus

**Group Mentoring:**
- Per-session rate
- 5-10 participants
- Monthly program structure

### Promotion Criteria

**To Junior Full-Time Instructor:**
- 50+ sessions delivered
- 4.5/5 average student rating
- 2+ courses developed
- Strong technical expertise

**To Instructor:**
- 150+ sessions delivered
- 4.6/5 average student rating
- 5+ courses owned
- Mentored 2+ junior instructors

**To Senior Instructor:**
- 300+ sessions delivered
- 4.7/5 average student rating
- 10+ courses owned
- Program leadership demonstrated
- Industry recognition

**To Lead Instructor:**
- 500+ sessions delivered
- 4.8/5 average student rating
- Strategic curriculum development
- Team management experience
- Business development contribution

## Requirements & Standards

### Teaching Requirements

**Minimum Standards:**
- Student satisfaction: 4/5 average
- Session completion: 95%+
- Material preparation: 100%
- Professional conduct: Excellent

**Continuous Improvement:**
- Quarterly teaching evaluations
- Peer observation (2x/year)
- Student feedback review
- Professional development (20 hours/year)

### Technical Requirements

**Expertise Levels:**
- **Intern Instructors:** Intermediate (can teach basics)
- **Part-Time:** Advanced (can teach intermediate)
- **Full-Time:** Expert (can teach advanced topics)
- **Senior/Lead:** Master (can teach anything, create new content)

**Certification (Preferred):**
- Relevant technical certifications
- Teaching certifications
- Industry experience
- Portfolio of work

### Professional Standards

**Code of Conduct:**
- Respect all students
- Maintain confidentiality
- Professional appearance
- Punctuality and reliability
- Continuous learning
- Ethical behavior

**Violations:**
- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: Suspension
- Third offense: Termination
- Serious violations: Immediate termination

## Payment & Administration

### Payment Schedule

**Session-Based:**
- Invoices submitted monthly
- Payment within 15 days
- Bank transfer or PayPal

**Full-Time:**
- Monthly salary (last working day)
- Quarterly bonuses (within 15 days of quarter end)
- Annual bonuses (within 30 days of year end)

### Tax & Legal

**Intern Instructors:**
- Payments included in internship stipend
- Taxes handled by Company

**Part-Time/Freelance:**
- Independent contractor status
- Responsible for own taxes
- Invoice required for payment
- VAT applicable (if registered)

**Full-Time:**
- Employee status
- Taxes withheld by Company
- Social security contributions
- Benefits included

### Invoicing Requirements

**Invoice Must Include:**
- Instructor name and tax ID
- Company details
- Invoice number and date
- Session details (date, type, duration)
- Hours worked (for hourly rates)
- Total amount
- Payment terms
- Bank details

## Intern-to-Instructor Pipeline

### Month 5-6: Teaching Introduction

**Preparation:**
- Complete "Teaching Fundamentals" course (8 hours)
- Shadow 3+ experienced instructors
- Co-teach 2 sessions with mentor
- Prepare first solo session

**First Sessions:**
- Start with familiar topics
- Smaller class sizes (5-10 students)
- Mentor observes and provides feedback
- Gradual complexity increase

**Support:**
- Weekly instructor meetings
- Peer feedback sessions
- Teaching resources library
- Mentor availability

### Month 7-12: Teaching Development

**Expansion:**
- Teach 10-20 sessions/month
- Develop new curriculum
- Larger class sizes (15-20 students)
- More complex topics

**Skill Building:**
- Advanced teaching techniques
- Curriculum design
- Student engagement strategies
- Assessment creation

**Evaluation:**
- Student feedback analysis
- Peer reviews
- Self-reflection
- Improvement plans

### Post-Internship: Career Decision

**Option 1: Full-Time Instructor**
- Transition to full-time teaching role
- Reduced or eliminated product development
- Focus on Academy growth
- Leadership opportunities

**Option 2: Hybrid Role**
- 50% product development
- 50% Academy instruction
- Best of both worlds
- Unique value proposition

**Option 3: Part-Time Instructor**
- Primary career elsewhere
- Teach on side (evenings/weekends)
- Maintain connection to Academy
- Flexible engagement

## Quality Assurance

### Student Feedback

**Collection:**
- After every session
- Anonymous surveys
- 5-point rating scale
- Open-ended comments

**Metrics:**
- Overall satisfaction
- Content quality
- Teaching effectiveness
- Pace and difficulty
- Instructor knowledge

**Action:**
- Less than 4.0: Immediate review and support
- 4.0-4.5: Continuous improvement
- Greater than 4.5: Best practices sharing

### Peer Review

**Process:**
- Quarterly peer observations
- Structured feedback form
- Constructive discussion
- Improvement planning

**Benefits:**
- Learn from each other
- Maintain standards
- Build community
- Share best practices

### Academy Lead Review

**Frequency:** Quarterly  
**Focus:**
- Teaching effectiveness
- Curriculum quality
- Student outcomes
- Professional development
- Career progression

## FAQ

**Q: Can I teach while still an intern?**  
A: Yes, starting in Month 5 if you meet performance requirements.

**Q: How much can I earn teaching during internship?**  
A: Additional teaching compensation varies based on number of sessions and performance (Months 5-6), with enhanced rates during extension period (Months 7-12).

**Q: Do I need teaching experience?**  
A: No, we provide full training and mentorship.

**Q: Can I teach part-time after leaving?**  
A: Yes, we encourage alumni to stay connected through teaching.

**Q: What if students rate me poorly?**  
A: We provide support and improvement plans. Everyone starts somewhere.

**Q: Can I develop my own courses?**  
A: Yes, we encourage curriculum innovation with proper approval.

**Q: Is there a path to full-time teaching?**  
A: Absolutely. Many of our lead instructors started as interns.

**Q: Can I teach remotely?**  
A: Yes, most sessions can be delivered remotely.
