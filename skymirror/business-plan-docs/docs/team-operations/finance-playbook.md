### Budget Process

#### Budget Template Structure

| Category | Line Items |
|----------|------------|
| **Revenue** | By product, by segment |
| **COGS** | Direct costs by product |
| **Gross Profit** | Revenue - COGS |
| **Operating Expenses** | |
| - Sales & Marketing | Headcount, campaigns, events |
| - R&D | Headcount, tools, infrastructure |
| - G&A | Headcount, facilities, professional services |
| **EBITDA** | Gross Profit - OpEx |
| **Capital Expenditure** | Equipment, software |
| **Headcount** | By department, by role |

#### Budget Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Approval Required |
|--------|-------------------|
| Under €5,000 | Department Head |
| €5,000-€25,000 | CFO |
| €25,000-€100,000 | CEO |
| Over €100,000 | Board |

|----------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Weekly Cash | Weekly | 13 weeks | Cash management |

#### Forecast Methodology

| Revenue Stream | Method |
|----------------|--------|
| SaaS (CheckMet, Traquiva) | Cohort-based, retention adjusted |

### Financial Metrics

#### Key Performance Indicators

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Revenue Growth** | YoY revenue increase | Over 100% Y1-Y2 |
| **Gross Margin** | Gross Profit / Revenue | Over 60% |
| **EBITDA Margin** | EBITDA / Revenue | Over 20% (Y3) |
| **Burn Rate** | Monthly cash consumption | Decreasing |
| **Runway** | Cash / Monthly Burn | Over 18 months |
| **CAC** | Sales & Marketing / New Customers | Under €5,000 |
| **LTV** | Avg Revenue × Lifetime | Over €15,000 |
| **LTV:CAC** | LTV / CAC | Over 3:1 |

#### SaaS Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Growing |
| **ARR** | Annual Recurring Revenue | €500K Y1 |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | (Starting + Expansion - Churn) / Starting | Over 100% |
| **Gross Retention** | (Starting - Churn) / Starting | Over 90% |
| **Churn Rate** | Lost ARR / Starting ARR | Under 10% |

## Financial Reporting

### Internal Reports

| Report | Frequency | Audience | Content |
|--------|-----------|----------|---------|
| Flash Report | Weekly | Leadership | Key metrics snapshot |
| P&L | Monthly | Leadership | Income statement |
| Balance Sheet | Monthly | CFO | Financial position |
| Cash Flow | Monthly | CFO | Cash movements |
| Variance Analysis | Monthly | Leadership | Budget vs. actual |
| Board Package | Quarterly | Board | Comprehensive review |

### Board Package Contents

1. **Executive Summary** (1 page)
   - Key highlights
   - Critical issues
   - Decisions needed

2. **Financial Performance** (3-4 pages)
   - P&L with variance analysis
   - Balance sheet
   - Cash flow
   - Key metrics dashboard

3. **Operational Metrics** (2-3 pages)
   - Product metrics
   - Customer metrics
   - Team metrics

4. **Strategic Updates** (2-3 pages)
   - Product roadmap progress
   - Market developments
   - Competitive landscape

5. **Appendix**
   - Detailed financials
   - Supporting data

### Investor Reporting

| Report | Frequency | Content |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Monthly Update | Monthly | Key metrics, highlights |
| Quarterly Report | Quarterly | Detailed performance |
| Annual Report | Annually | Comprehensive review |
