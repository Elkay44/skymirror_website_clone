## Week 1: Onboarding & Foundations

### Theme: Orientation, Tools, and Context
**Primary Goal:** Interns are fully set up, understand expectations, and make their first contribution.

### Week 1 Overview

| Day | Focus | Key Activities |
|-----|-------|----------------|
| **Monday** | Orientation & Setup | Welcome, tool setup, team introductions |
| **Tuesday** | Technical Foundations | Dev environment, codebase walkthrough |
| **Wednesday** | Workflow & Processes | Git/GitHub, Jira/Trello, first ticket |
| **Thursday** | First Contribution | Complete and submit first PR |
| **Friday** | Review & Reflection | Week review, sprint demo, retrospective |
