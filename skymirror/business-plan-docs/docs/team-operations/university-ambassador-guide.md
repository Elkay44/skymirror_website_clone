# University Ambassador Guide: "Campus CEO" Program

## Program Overview

The Skymirror Academy University Ambassador Program (UAP) is a prestigious leadership initiative designed to empower student leaders to represent Skymirror at their respective universities. As an Ambassador, you are the "Campus CEO" of Skymirror Academy, responsible for building a community of future engineers and AI specialists.

**Objective:** Bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice by bringing Skymirror's world-class training to your campus.

## üöÄ Core Responsibilities

How you will spend your time as an Ambassador:

### **Digital Presence (Ongoing)**
*   Share weekly Academy updates, student success stories, and tech insights on your LinkedIn and university WhatsApp/Telegram groups.
*   Represent Skymirror in university tech forums.

### **On-Campus Events (1 per month)**
*   Host "Intro to Coding" or "Future of AI" workshops (Skymirror provides the deck and curriculum).
*   Organize "Career in Tech" Q&A sessions.
*   Secure speaking slots for Skymirror leadership at university conferences.

### **Lead Generation & Conversion**
*   Identify students who are struggling with their curriculum or looking for practical skills.
*   Guide them through the application process for the Academy.
*   Use your unique referral link/QR code for all registrations.
